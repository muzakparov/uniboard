What Is PowerFAIDS?

PowerFAIDS is a comprehensive, customizable software solution that automates your
financial aid process by:
  • Performing need-analysis calculations;
  • Communicating with students via email, letter, or through the Net Partner student portal;
  • Running complex packaging calculations;
  • Transmitting grant and loan data;
  • Processing federal funds;
  • Packaging awards and originating loans; and
  • Automating reports.

PowerFAIDS allows you to efficiently and equitably administer financial aid, eliminating hours of paperwork and allowing you more time for student interaction.

What Is PowerFAIDS?

PowerFAIDS was developed by seasoned
financial aid experts at the College Board. We've been supporting financial aid since the development of the field over 60 years ago.

PowerFAIDS is backed by independent research, experienced field staff, and insights from nearly 4,000 College Board member institutions, committees, and advisory groups.

Manage Compliance and Reports

Federal regulations on Title IV Pell Grants and loans change rapidly, and keeping up with the federal requirements is a challenge.

The College Board updates PowerFAIDS to ensure that your financial aid operations remain compliant.

PowerFAIDS is also fully compatible with federal systems such as the Central Processing System (CPS), used by the U.S. Department of Education's Office of Federal Student Aid, and the Common Origination and Disbursement (COD) System.

Manage Compliance and Reports

PowerFAIDS allows you to access, create,
receive, and send federal financial aid data, including:

  • Loading, processing, and adjusting data in Institutional Student Information Records (ISIRs) from the CPS;
  • Viewing National Student Loan Data System data; and
  • Transmitting loan files in XML format suitable for automatic transmit to COD. Alternative loan files can also be transmitted using the CommonLine format.

Manage Compliance and Reports

PowerFAIDS also helps you serve your institution's low-income populations by processing federal Pell Grants with:

  • Automated Pell Grant calculations, reporting, and reconciliation; and
  • Easy communication with the U.S. Department of Education's COD system.
